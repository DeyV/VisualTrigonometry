Touch Mathematics is a collection
of free, web browser-based apps
that aim to make math concepts intuitive
for learners of all ages.

History

Touch Mathematics was created by Matthew Trost in 2011.

It is an outgrowth of "Touch Trigonometry", a Flash visualization he created in 2010, as seen on Reddit and elsewhere.
